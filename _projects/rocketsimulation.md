The goal of this project was to independently create a MATLAB simulation of the flight of a water bottle rocket based on concepts such as the Rocket Equation that I had learned in class. We then aligned the simulation to data collected on a bottle rocket, accelerometer, and RTC with our simulation to generate more accurate results. After aligning the curves to be as close as possible, I then create an optimization script to iterate through all possible values of initial fuel (water) and height reached to find the best ratio of fuel to height. </br>
